About This Tool

This converter allows you to easily convert rich text and tables from Word or other tools into a GPT-friendly format, and vice versa. It's particularly useful when working with AI services like GPT that only accept plain text input. You can convert your formatted text and tables to a GPT-friendly format, send it to the AI, and then convert the AI's response back to rich text and formatted tables.

FAQ

Q: Why would I need to convert between Rich Text/Tables and GPT-friendly format?

A: Many AI services, including GPT, only accept plain text input. Converting your formatted text and tables to a GPT-friendly format allows you to preserve formatting in a plain text format that's compatible with these AI services.

Q: Is my data safe when using this tool?

A: Yes, all conversions happen in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

Q: Can I use this tool offline?

A: Once the page is loaded, the converter will work offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to load the page initially.
